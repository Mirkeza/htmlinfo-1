Lisbon - The City of Cristiano Ronaldo

Lisbon is the capital and the largest city of Portugal, known for its rich history, architecture, and football culture. The city is located on the western coast of the Iberian Peninsula, facing the Atlantic Ocean.

One of the most famous figures associated with Lisbon is Cristiano Ronaldo, who was born in Madeira but rose to fame in Lisbon while playing for Sporting CP. Today, he is considered one of the greatest footballers of all time.

"Football has given me everything, and it all started in Lisbon." – Cristiano Ronaldo

From its picturesque streets to the famous Belém Tower, Lisbon is a city that blends tradition and modernity effortlessly.
